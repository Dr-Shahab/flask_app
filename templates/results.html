Interpretation

How these results should be used

MolScore-PK reports calculated molecular descriptors, medicinal chemistry rules, alert-based filtering, and descriptor-driven prioritization to support comparative profiling and early-stage compound triage. These outputs are not experimental PK values, not target-specific activity predictions, and should be interpreted as screening support rather than definitive decision endpoints.
